The Best Movies According to Aman

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Pursuit of Happyness

"Pursuit of Happiness" is a very warm and above all, a very inspiring story about family, determination, and never letting go of one's dream

The Shawshank Redemption

It is one of eternal hope. Andy Dufresne, played by Tim Robbins, has been sent to prison for a crime he did not commit. But he never loses hope.

The Green Mile

The Green Mile is a 1999 American fantasy drama film written and directed by Frank Darabont and based on Stephen King's 1996 novel of the same name.
